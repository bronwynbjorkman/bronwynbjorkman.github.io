The Linguistics Lounge

Some of you may not know this, but on the third floor of the linguistics building there is a lounge. In it are couches, a blackboard, a sink, and a fridge. It's a good place to go to read, particularly if you want to be distracted by conversations about linguistics.

Tutoring Information

If you're feeling a little overwhelmed by one of your courses, SLUM can put you in touch with a qualified tutor for the area in question. Our tutors are generally upper-year undergraduates who can meet with you one-on-one or in small groups to go over concepts you are covering in your course.

To request a tutor, please email SLUM at least three days before the time you would like to meet with your tutor (we can't guarantee to find you a tutor at the last minute...). Please indicate which course you would like help for and your availability (it makes it easier for us if you give us a few different times when you are available — the tutors are busy, too...). Finally, please note that the cost for tutoring is $15/hour — this is the same rate as the McGill Tutoring Service, but they often don't have tutors for lingustics courses, hence this service. Please email us for any additional information or to request a tutor.
