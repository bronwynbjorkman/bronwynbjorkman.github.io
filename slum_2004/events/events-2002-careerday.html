Linguistics Career Day  March 19, 2003
Brown Student Services Building, rm. 3001

SLUM organised a Career Day in collaboration with CAPS. Two events were scheduled:

Informal "Bag Lunch" Panel
Panelists were asked to talk about their career path, current employers and how the found their position. Presentations were followed by a question/chat period.
Panelists:
Vivianne Philips, M.A. Linguistics, Copy Editor, Marketel
Jason McDevitt (McGill alumnus), Nuance
Prof. Charles Boberg, Undergraduate Advisor, Linguistics Department, McGill University
Prof. Glyne Piggott, Graduate Program Director, Linguistics Department, McGill University

Workshop on Graduate School and the Job Market

David Ainsworth, Graduate Student Advisor at CAPS, provided insight on what grad school really is, how to decide whether or not to attend grad school and what it's like to go through a graduate program.

A mini 'what can I do with my B.A.?' workshop was also offered.
