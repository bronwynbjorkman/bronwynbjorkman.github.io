Welcome to the new SLUM website!

Here you'll find notices of upcoming Linguistics department and SLUM events, related lectures, and links to interesting research and resource sites.

SLUM, as the undergraduate linguistics society, organizes social and academic events, acts as undergraduate representatives to the Department and keeps students up to date on departmental activities. We also offer a tutoring service for all undergraduate linguistics courses. See the tutoring page for details.

Questions? Comments? Wanna help out? write SLUM at slumlinguistics@po-box.mcgill.ca.
